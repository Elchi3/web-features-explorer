Lighting estimation

Limited availability
Tags: api Groups: WebXR

The XRLightProbe and XRLightEstimate objects represent real-world environmental lighting conditions during a WebXR session. To learn more, see XRFrame: getLightEstimate() method, XRLightEstimate, XRLightEstimate: primaryLightDirection property, XRLightEstimate: primaryLightIntensity property, XRLightEstimate: sphericalHarmonicsCoefficients property, XRLightProbe, XRLightProbe: probeSpace property, XRLightProbe: reflectionchange event, XRSession: preferredReflectionFormat property, XRSession: requestLightProbe() method, XRWebGLBinding: getReflectionCubeMap() method at MDN.
